Upper Amenfi Rural Bank Recieves Ghana Club 100 (2011) award.

GHANA CLUB 100 AWARDS (2011 EDITION)
 Ladies and Gentlemen the bank again made it to the Ghana Club 100 for the third time in 2011 rankings, moving from 56th position in 2009 to the 42nd position in 2010 and to the 36th position in 2011.
We once again dedicate this Award to the honour of the board, Shareholders, Management and Staff, and all stakeholders and hope that with the support of everybody, we can move further up the ladder in 2012.
